LA is facing it's worst wildfires in history

By Kennedy French

Four fires have broken out in LA county destroying thousands of homes and buildings.

  • Palisades Fire
  • Eaton Fire
  • Hurst Fire
  • Auto Fire

The Santa Ana winds picked up in the last week encouraging fires to spread through Los Angeles Rapidly. The biggest of the fires in the Palisades has burned more than 23,707 acres making it one of the most destructive fires in California's history.

Source: National Weather Service

Just a day after making progress containing the Eaton fires and protecting Brentwood and Encino from the Palisades fire, Los Angeles braces for another set of winds that could threaten more communities. Winds up to 65mph are expected Monday, with even stronger winds arriving early Tuesday morning and throughout Wednesday.
